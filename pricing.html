Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the pricing?

All pricing includes design, development, testing, and deployment. Additional services like content creation, premium stock images, or third-party integrations may incur extra costs.

How long does a project typically take?

Basic projects take 1-2 weeks, Standard projects 2-4 weeks, and Premium projects 4-6 weeks. Timelines may vary based on project complexity and client responsiveness.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer maintenance plans starting at $50/month for updates, security monitoring, and minor changes. This is optional but recommended for long-term website health.
